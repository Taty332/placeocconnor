Discover Place O'Connor




In the Heart of Huntingdon

Huntingdon is a charming town in Montérégie, offering a peaceful, natural lifestyle— ideal for families and those seeking tranquility—while remaining close to major cities for occasional travel. Place O'Connor enjoys a central location with easy access to various services and amenities.

Shops and Services Nearby

Place O'Connor is ideally situated near a variety of shops and services, making daily life convenient:
- Huntingdon Town Hall : administrative services, 3 minutes by car.
- Caisse Desjardins : banking services, 5 minutes by car.
- Marché Richelieu : grocery shopping for all your needs, 4 minutes by car.
- The White Rabbit : a popular local restaurant for family meals, 6 minutes on foot.

Community Services

The Huntingdon Training Center, located just 5 minutes away on foot, is dedicated to literacy development, offering workshops in reading, writing, and basic math in both French and English, contributing to essential skill development for the local community.

Education

Huntingdon offers several schooling options for families:
- Heritage Elementary School (2 minutes on foot) : a bilingual elementary school providing quality education.
- Huntingdon Adult Education and Community Centre (HAECC) (2 minutes by car) : an education center for young adults and adults, offering a variety of programs and community activities.

Natural Attractions

The Châteauguay River, which flows through Huntingdon, offers wonderful opportunities for outdoor activities, perfect for relaxation or recreation. Residents can enjoy water sports such as canoeing, kayaking, and paddleboarding. The area also features hiking trails and bike paths, perfect for exploring the surrounding natural beauty.

Directly behind Place O'Connor is the beautiful Prince Arthur Park, a peaceful green space. The park features benches for relaxation, quiet areas to enjoy nature, as well as a playground and splash pad for children, making it an ideal spot for families and nature enthusiasts alike.

Our Community

Beyond its ideal location and modern amenities, Place O'Connor invites you to become part of a welcoming community. Enjoy the friendly ambiance of Huntingdon and engage in a town rich in history and local vibrancy. Huntingdon is also known for its community events and festivals, including:
- Huntingdon Fall Festival : un événement annuel célébrant l'automne avec des activités familiales, des marchés artisanaux et de la musique.
- Huntingdon County Farmer's Market : un marché saisonnier où les producteurs locaux offrent des produits frais et artisanaux.
- National Holiday : célébrée chaque année le 24 juin, rassemblant la communauté avec spectacles, feux d'artifice et activités familiales.
By choosing to live at Place O'Connor, you embrace a harmonious lifestyle that combines historical heritage with modern comfort, all within a vibrant and welcoming community.
